Biography

Xiaolei Fang is an Associate Professor in the Edward P. Fitts Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering at North Carolina State University.He earned a Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering with a minor in Machine Learning and an M.S. in Statistics from the Georgia Institute of Technology, and a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Science and Technology Beijing.
